Privacy Policy – CuteAnimalsDailyClips

Last updated: 09/02/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CuteAnimalsDailyClips (the “App”) handles information.

1. Data we process

The App may process a video file and an optional caption provided by you for the purpose of uploading content to your TikTok account.

2. Personal data

The App does not intentionally collect personal data from end users. The App is used by the account owner for publishing content.

3. Credentials

Authentication is handled via TikTok’s authorization mechanisms. Do not share your credentials with others.

4. Data retention

The App may temporarily handle files during upload. No long-term storage is intended beyond what is required for the upload workflow.

5. Third parties

TikTok’s services are used to publish content. Their privacy policies apply to your TikTok account usage.

6. Contact

If you have questions, contact the owner of the TikTok account using the App.
